1 What is unique about the Norwegian Lundehund's physical anatomy compared to other dog breeds, and what was it historically used for? The Norwegian Lundehund is unique due to its six toes on each foot, prick ears that can fold closed backwards or forwards at will, and a distinctive ability to tip its head backward until it touches the backbone. Historically, this breed was used for wrestling and retrieving live Puffins from steep vertical cliff crevices.
